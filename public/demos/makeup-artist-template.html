Artistry FAQ

✦

Answers to common scheduling and styling inquiries

How far in advance should I reserve a bridal styling?
We highly recommend booking bridal packages 6 to 12 months in advance to secure availability, particularly during high summer wedding seasons.
Do you provide on-site travel for destination weddings?
Yes. Our studio offers travel services internationally. Travel compensation rates, flights, and lodging requirements are calculated individually based on the location.
What brand cosmetics do you use in your styling kit?
We use strictly premium, hypoallergenic, professional brands (such as Chanel, Dior, Armani Beauty, and Tom Ford) designed for high durability and digital photography filters.
Is a trial session included with bridal packages?
Yes. All bridal packages include a complimentary trial session 4–6 weeks before the wedding date so you can experience the look and request any refinements.
