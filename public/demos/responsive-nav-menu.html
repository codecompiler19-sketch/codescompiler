Hamburger Menu
in Action

Resize the browser window to under 700px to see the hamburger menu appear. Click it to open/close the slide-in navigation panel.

🔧 Features demonstrated:

  • Sticky header that stays at top on scroll
  • Smooth hamburger → X icon animation
  • Slide-in sidebar from the right
  • Overlay backdrop when menu is open
  • Click outside / overlay to close
