How to Play & Game Instructions

  • 1
    Select Difficulty: Adjust the difficulty settings from the sidebar. High difficulty levels offer faster spawning moles but higher scoring potential.
  • 2
    Whack Moles: Tap or click on emerging moles as quickly as possible. Whacking a mole yields 100 points multiplied by your current combo multiplier.
  • 3
    Combo Multiplier: Maintain consecutive hits to increase your combo meter (up to x5). Missing a mole or clicking an empty hole resets your combo meter back to x1.
  • 4
    Time Penalty: Clicking on an empty hole or missing active moles will deduct 1 second from the clock, so whack with precision!
  • 5
    Retro Synthesizer Sounds: Sound effects are auto-generated natively via your browser's Web Audio API. You can toggle them using the sidebar button.
